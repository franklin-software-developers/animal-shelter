Welcome to FSD Animal Shelter, where every pet deserves a loving home.

Why you should adopt:

- Save money while you save a life
- A wide variety of pets to choose from
- Give an adult pet a home
- Contribute in the fight against factory-style breeding of pets
"Beyond just helping an animal in need, you're giving a rescue an opportunity to find their voice; to be themselves and get a second chance to become a dog beyond the walls of shelter or rescue. You truly give them the keys to start anew in a life where second chances can often be hard to come by" (ASPCA).
